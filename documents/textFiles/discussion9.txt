I got my internship at the end of my sophomore year with only two CS classes under my belt.

Because of that internship, I managed to get another internship junior year. By the end of my senior year, I had offers all over the USA.

Go to the career fair. Go to the business career fair as well. Talk to every single recruiter. If you have a cool project or app, print it out and use it as a starting point.

Just go to the career fair. It's the easiest way to line up a job in the future. The same companies drop in every year. Get feedback from the recruiter, then come back the the next year with more for experience.
